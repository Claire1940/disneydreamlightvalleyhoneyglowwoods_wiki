<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Gateway Activation">

Unlocking the portal to the new biomes requires completing a short introductory sequence in your main Valley. Follow these steps to establish the permanent link between your home and the Honeyglow Woods.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Retrieve the Mailbox Package">
    Check your in-game mailbox to find a mysterious package filled with childhood keepsakes and a unique Tree Sprout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plant the Tree Sprout">
    Dig a spot in your Valley, plant the Tree Sprout, and water it. It will immediately grow into a magical glowing tree gateway.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Place the Magical Gateway">
    Use the Furniture menu to place the gateway anywhere in your Valley or on the base game Floating Islands. You can move or remove this gateway at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Step Through the Portal">
    Interact with the gateway to enter the Honeyglow Woods, where a thick, mysterious fog has settled over the land.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Gateway Placement">
Ensure you place the gateway in an easily accessible area of your Valley. While you can reposition it later, having it near a Fast Travel wishing well makes early-game backtracking much more efficient.
</Callout>

</Section>

Upon entering the woods, your primary goal in Chapter 1 is to locate Winnie the Pooh and help him address the strange fog that has separated the forest residents. This chapter introduces the first of three major characters featured in the expansion.

| Character | Role in Chapter 1 | Unlock Focus |

Once you meet Pooh, you will work together to uncover clues around the central landmark: the massive Everoak Tree. This central hub holds the key to the region's magic and must be restored step-by-step.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

Chapter 1 introduces the foundational mechanics of Beekeeping. By restoring the Busy Bees' Houses, you can begin harvesting Golden Honey, which is the primary resource for new recipes and themed furniture sets.

| Step | Action | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Locate a Busy Bees' House | Establish the honey collection point |
| **2** | Plant Flowers Nearby | Bees pollinate flowers to generate honey |
| **3** | Collect Golden Honey | Use honey for crafting and cooking |
| **4** | Boost Flower Growth | Active bee houses speed up nearby flower growth |

<Callout type="info" title="Beekeeping Tip">
You can move Busy Bees' Houses to other biomes outside the expansion. This allows you to bring Honeyglow Beekeeping directly into your main Valley or custom farming layouts.
</Callout>
