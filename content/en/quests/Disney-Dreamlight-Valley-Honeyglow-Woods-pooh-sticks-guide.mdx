<Section icon={Target} title="Pooh Sticks Rules and Gameplay Mechanics">

Pooh Sticks is a relaxed, physics-based racing mini-game played on the edge of the Gloommeadow bridge. It offers a cozy break from farming and questing while serving as an excellent tool for leveling up your friendships.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Your Competitors">
    Approach the Gloommeadow bridge and interact with the starting prompt. You will assemble a group of four villagers to participate in the upcoming heat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Racing Object">
    Choose a lightweight object from your inventory or the nearby woodland floor. Different organic materials have slight variances in how they interact with the water currents.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Drop and Drift">
    On the starting signal, all four participants drop their chosen sticks or objects simultaneously over the upstream side of the bridge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Track the Downstream Finish">
    Run to the downstream side of the bridge and watch the floating items drift. The first object to cross the designated finish line downstream wins the round.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Warning">
Keep a few slots open in your inventory before starting a match. While the mini-game does not require expensive gear, having native forest materials on hand makes selecting your racing object faster.
</Callout>

</Section>
