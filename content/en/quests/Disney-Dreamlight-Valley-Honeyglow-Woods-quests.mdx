<Section icon={Target} title="Main Story Walkthrough and Character Unlocks">

The narrative core of the Honeyglow Woods expansion revolves around a strange, melancholic fog that has separated the residents of the forest. Your primary objective is to reunite Winnie the Pooh, Eeyore, and Piglet while investigating the Everoak Tree.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Rescue Winnie the Pooh">
    Upon entering Drowsybloom Acre, you will find Pooh Bear searching for clues. Complete his introductory tasks involving cloud-watching and hide-and-seek to restore his confidence and clear the initial fog layer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Gloommeadow Search">
    Progress into the misty, swampy Gloommeadow to find Eeyore. This questline focuses on searching for his missing tail and restoring the Honeyglow bridge to unlock the Pooh Sticks mini-game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Braveheart Grove Courage">
    Piglet is located in the deeper Braveheart Grove. You must help him find his courage by completing tasks related to "puddle-jumping" and clearing the densest parts of the magical fog.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Everoak Tree Mystery">
    Once all three friends are reunited, you will gain access to the interior of the Everoak Tree. Solve the final puzzles within the tree to mend the land and permanently restore the Woods.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

A unique feature of the **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods quests** is the introduction of a functional beekeeping system. This isn't just for decoration; it is a vital resource loop located primarily in the Nectar Apiary.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Busy Bees' House">
    The central hub for honey production. Must be placed near compatible flowers to function.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Golden Honey">
    A rare resource used for high-tier recipes and Honeyglow-themed furniture crafting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pollination Boost">
    A well-maintained bee house increases the growth speed of all nearby flowers and crops.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

To master beekeeping, you must restore the Busy Bees' Houses scattered throughout the Woods. Once repaired, you can move these houses to any biome, including your main Valley, to create a sprawling honey farm.

| Resource | Acquisition Method | Primary Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Golden Honey** | Harvested from Busy Bees' Houses | Cooking and Crafting |
| **Honeycombs** | Found in the Nectar Apiary | Material for Bee Houses |
| **Wildflowers** | Foraged in Honeyglow Woods | Pollination and Decor |
| **Honeyleaf** | Foraged near the Everoak Tree | Cozy Honeyleaf Rug |

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Tip">
Place a variety of different colored flowers around a single Busy Bees' House. The diversity of flora increases the yield frequency of Golden Honey compared to using only one type of flower.
</Callout>

</Section>

Beyond cooking, the crafting station receives a significant update with the Honeyglow Furniture set. These items feature warm, amber-lit textures and organic, woodland shapes that fit perfectly with a "cottagecore" aesthetic.

**Key Craftable Items:**
- **Honeypot Lamp**: A glowing pot that provides soft, warm light.
- **Cozy Honeyleaf Rug**: A nature-themed floor covering for indoor spaces.
- **Honeyglow Bridge (Miniature)**: A decorative version of the Pooh Sticks bridge.
- **Busy Bees' House**: Additional houses can be crafted after the first one is restored.

<Callout type="warning" title="Limited Resources">
Golden Honey takes time to regenerate. Avoid using all your honey on recipes if you are currently working on a quest that requires crafting the Honeyglow Furniture set, as those pieces require significant amounts of honey.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Side Activities: Pooh Sticks and Critters">

While the main **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods quests** focus on the story, the expansion includes cozy side activities that enhance friendship progression.

**The Pooh Sticks Mini-Game:**
Located at the bridge in the Gloommeadow, this activity allows you to compete with three other villagers. Each participant drops an object into the river, and the first one to reach the finish line downstream wins.
- **Location:** Gloommeadow Bridge.
- **Participants:** You and three villagers.
- **Benefit:** Significant boost to Friendship Levels for all participants.

**Hedgehog Critters:**
Honeyglow Woods introduces a family of colorful hedgehogs. These critters are the first to feature unique Photo Mode poses, making them perfect companions for screenshots.
