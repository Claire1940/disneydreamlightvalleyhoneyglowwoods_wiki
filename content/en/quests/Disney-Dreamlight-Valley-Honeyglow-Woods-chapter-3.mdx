<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Chapter 3 Progression Walkthrough">

Navigating **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods chapter 3** requires a specific sequence of actions to clear the fog blocking Braveheart Grove and rescue Piglet. Follow these structured steps to complete the core story arc efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate 'Courage in the Woods'">
    Speak with Winnie the Pooh near the Gloommeadow bridge. He will express concern about Piglet, who has run deep into the fog-covered Braveheart Grove out of fear. Pooh provides you with a special Tree Sprout recipe.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Harvest Golden Honey">
    Set up your Busy Bees' Houses in Drowsybloom Acre. Surround each house with at least six local flowers to begin the pollination process. Collect the generated Golden Honey once the production cycle completes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft the Honeyglow Ward">
    Head to any crafting station and combine your gathered Golden Honey with Dreamlight and softwood to craft the Honeyglow Ward. This magical item is required to pierce the dense fog.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dispel the Braveheart Grove Fog">
    Approach the northern barrier in Gloommeadow. Interact with the misty archway and place the Honeyglow Ward into the quest slot to permanently clear the fog and unlock Braveheart Grove.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Rescue Piglet and Restore the Everoak">
    Find Piglet hiding inside a hollow log in Braveheart Grove. Complete his courage-building mini-game, return him safely to Pooh, and place his new home to complete the main chapter storyline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
Do not attempt to enter Braveheart Grove before crafting the Honeyglow Ward. Entering the fog without the ward active will teleport your character back to the Drowsybloom Acre starting point.
</Callout>

</Section>
