<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Steps to Complete Piglet's First Quest">

The first quest, "A Small Friend in a Big Wood," is essential for bringing Piglet into your Valley. Follow these steps to ensure you don't miss any clues hidden in the Braveheart Grove.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Rustling Bushes">
    After entering Braveheart Grove, look for three specific bushes that are shaking. Piglet is hiding in one of them, but he will move if you approach too loudly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Childhood Keepsakes">
    You will need to find three items: a small blue balloon, a piece of hay, and a worn-out scarf. These are scattered around the Gloommeadow bridge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Craft the 'Courage Charm'">
    Use the crafting station to combine the keepsakes with 5 Golden Honey. This item is used to convince Piglet that the woods are safe.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Build Piglet's Home">
    Place Piglet's house in the Valley. It costs 5,000 Star Coins to construct via the Scrooge McDuck sign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Placement Tip">
Place Piglet's home near a water source or the Gloommeadow bridge to trigger unique animations where he watches the water or plays with his friends.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Piglet's Role in Beekeeping and Crafting">

Piglet isn't just a resident; he is a functional part of the Honeyglow Woods ecosystem. His specialty lies in the **Nectar Apiary**, where he helps players manage the Busy Bees’ Houses.

When Piglet is assigned the "Gardening" or "Foraging" role, he has a chance to find extra **Golden Honey** when you harvest from the Busy Bees' Houses. This is critical for crafting the new Honeyglow furniture set, which requires significant amounts of honey and hardwood.
