<Section icon={Shield} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Guide">

Beekeeping is the primary new resource system introduced in this expansion. Generating **Golden Honey** allows you to craft high-value furniture and cook delicious new recipes.

| Step | Action | Practical Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Progress the main story | Unlocks the **Busy Bees' House** recipe |
| **2** | Place a Busy Bees' House | Establishes your honey harvesting node |
| **3** | Plant flowers nearby | Bees pollinate flowers, speeding up their growth |
| **4** | Harvest Golden Honey | Collect honey periodically from the house |

<Callout type="info" title="Apiary Efficiency">
The more flowers you plant directly adjacent to a Busy Bees' House, the faster it will produce Golden Honey. Furthermore, fully pollinated houses boost the growth speed of those surrounding flowers, creating a highly efficient farming loop.
</Callout>

Once you have harvested enough Golden Honey, you can head to any cooking station to prepare the new themed recipes:

- **Warm Milk & Honey**: 1x Milk, 1x Golden Honey (Cozy energy drink)
- **Honeycrunch Bar**: 1x Wheat, 1x Sugarcane, 1x Golden Honey (Sweet snack)
- **Pooh's Birthday Cake**: 1x Wheat, 1x Butter, 1x Egg, 1x Golden Honey (Character-themed dessert)

You can also craft exclusive decorative items like the **Honeypot Lamp**, **Cozy Honeyleaf Rug**, and various pieces of **Honeyglow Furniture** to decorate your Valley. Busy Bees' Houses can also be moved out of the expansion biomes and placed anywhere in your main Valley.

</Section>

### Playing Pooh Sticks
Head to the wooden bridge in the **Gloommeadow** to access the Pooh Sticks mini-game. 

1. Gather a group of up to four villagers at the bridge.
2. Each participant selects an object to drop into the river.
3. Watch the objects float downstream; the first object to cross the finish line wins.
4. Completing games of Pooh Sticks awards substantial friendship points to all participating villagers.

<Callout type="tip" title="Hedgehog Critters">
When exploring the new biomes, look out for the colorful family of hedgehogs. Feeding them their favorite flowers unlocks them as companions and grants special poses for your in-game Photo Mode.
