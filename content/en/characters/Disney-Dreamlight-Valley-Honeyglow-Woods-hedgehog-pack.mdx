  <Step num="2" title="Plant the Tree Sprout">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter the Gateway">

**The Pooh Sticks Mini-Game:**
Located at the bridge in the Gloommeadow, Pooh Sticks is a relaxing way to build friendship. You can invite up to four villagers to participate. Each participant drops an object into the river, and the first one to emerge on the other side of the bridge wins. This activity provides significant Friendship Level experience and is a great way to level up the new residents quickly.

<Callout type="tip" title="Friendship Tip">
Playing Pooh Sticks during rainy weather in the game can trigger unique dialogue and potentially rare item drops from the river.
</Callout>
