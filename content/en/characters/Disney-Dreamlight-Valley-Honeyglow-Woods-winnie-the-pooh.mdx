<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

Beekeeping is the premier resource system introduced in this Adventure Pack. By utilizing **Busy Bees' Houses**, you can automate the production of **Golden Honey**, a vital ingredient used for cooking high-energy recipes and crafting beautiful Honeyglow-themed furniture.

To maximize your honey production, you must understand the relationship between bee placement and surrounding flora.

| Mechanic | How It Works | Player Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Busy Bees' House** | The central structure where bees live and store honey. | Acts as the collection point for harvesting Golden Honey. |
| **Flower Placement** | Arrange wild or cultivated flowers directly around the bee houses. | Power up the pollination speed to generate honey faster. |
| **Growth Boost** | A fully active, pollinated Busy Bees' House emits a passive aura. | Increases the growth speed of all other nearby flowers. |
| **Portability** | Bee houses can be picked up in furniture mode. | Allows you to move your beekeeping setups to any base game biome. |

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Step 1: Place House">
    Set up your Busy Bees' House in an open area with plenty of flat ground for landscaping.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Step 2: Plant Flowers">
    Surround the house with various colorful flowers. More flowers mean faster honey accumulation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Step 3: Harvest Honey">
    Check back regularly to collect Golden Honey, which can be stored or used immediately in crafting.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Strategy">
Mix different species of flowers around your Busy Bees' House. Not only does this create a beautiful garden aesthetic, but it also optimizes the passive growth speed bonus applied to your surrounding agricultural plots.
</Callout>

### Pooh Sticks & Side Activities
To play Pooh Sticks, head to the wooden bridge located in the **Gloommeadow** biome. 

- **The Rules**: Gather up to four villagers on the bridge. Each participant drops a stick or object into the river. The first object to float out from under the opposite side of the bridge and cross the finish line wins.
- **The Reward**: Playing this daily mini-game provides a massive boost to the friendship levels of all three participating villagers, making it the most efficient way to level up your new neighbors.
