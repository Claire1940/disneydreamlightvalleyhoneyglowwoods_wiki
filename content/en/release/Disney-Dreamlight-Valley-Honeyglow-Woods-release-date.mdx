<Section icon={Swords} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

Beekeeping is the core gameplay loop introduced on the **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods release date**. This system allows you to produce **Golden Honey**, a high-value ingredient used for top-tier recipes and furniture crafting.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Place a Busy Bees’ House">
    Craft or obtain a **Busy Bees’ House** and place it in any biome. It works best in areas with high flower density.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Surround with Flowers">
    Plant or move flowers within the radius of the house. The bees will pollinate these flowers, which speeds up their growth and generates honey.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Harvest Golden Honey">
    Check the house periodically to collect **Golden Honey**. A fully pollinated house produces honey significantly faster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Crafting Utility">
Golden Honey is required for the **Honeyglow Furniture set**, which includes the popular **Honeypot Lamp** and **Cozy Honeyleaf Rug**.
</Callout>
