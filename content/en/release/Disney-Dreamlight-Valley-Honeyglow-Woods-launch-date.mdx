<Section icon={Map} title="Exploring the Four New Biomes">

Honeyglow Woods is not just a single forest; it is divided into four distinct areas, each with its own atmosphere, resources, and ecological challenges. The mystery of the Everoak Tree ties these regions together, as the tree's health directly impacts the surrounding land.

- **Drowsybloom Acre**: A sun-drenched meadow filled with vibrant flowers. This is the starting area where you will learn the basics of the expansion.
- **Gloommeadow**: A misty, marsh-like region where Eeyore resides. It features the iconic bridge used for Pooh Sticks.
- **Braveheart Grove**: A dense, blooming woodland that requires courage to navigate. It is the furthest point from the entrance and holds deep story secrets.
- **Nectar Apiary**: A hidden zone filled with giant honeycombs and the source of the world's Golden Honey.

| Biome Name | Key Resource | Unique Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Drowsybloom Acre** | Wildflowers | Starter gateway location |
| **Gloommeadow** | Marsh Water | Pooh Sticks bridge |
| **Braveheart Grove** | Ancient Wood | Hidden fog puzzles |
| **Nectar Apiary** | Golden Honey | Busy Bees' House hub |

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Farming">
The Busy Bees' Houses can be moved from the Nectar Apiary to any other biome, including your main Valley. This allows you to set up honey production wherever it fits your aesthetic.
</Callout>

</Section>
