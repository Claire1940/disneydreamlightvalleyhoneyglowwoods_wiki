<Section icon={Swords} title="Beekeeping Mechanics and Honey Recipes">

The core gameplay loop of Honeyglow Woods revolves around the Busy Bees’ Houses. This new system allows players to produce Golden Honey, a versatile ingredient used for high-tier recipes and the "Honeyglow" furniture set.

**How Beekeeping Works:**
1.  **Place a Busy Bees’ House:** These can be crafted or earned through quests.
2.  **Surround with Flowers:** The type and quantity of flowers nearby determine the speed and quality of honey production.
3.  **Pollination Boost:** Having a house active increases the growth speed of any flowers planted in its immediate radius.
4.  **Harvest Golden Honey:** Once the meter is full, you can harvest the honey for use in cooking and crafting.

| Recipe Name | Ingredients | Energy Bonus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warm Milk & Honey** | Milk, Golden Honey | **1,200** |
| **Honeycrunch Bar** | Wheat, Sugarcane, Golden Honey | **1,850** |
| **Pooh’s Birthday Cake** | Wheat, Egg, Butter, Golden Honey | **2,400** |
| **Honey-Glazed Salmon** | Salmon, Soya, Golden Honey | **2,100** |

<Callout type="success" title="Beekeeping Tip">
You can move Busy Bees’ Houses back to the main Valley biomes. This allows you to set up honey farms in the Meadow or Plaza, integrating the expansion mechanics into your daily routine.
</Callout>
