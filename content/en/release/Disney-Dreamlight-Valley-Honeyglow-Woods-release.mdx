<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide: How to Start the Adventure">

Once you have purchased the expansion, the **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods release** content does not unlock automatically in your menu. Instead, it follows a narrative path that begins right in your Valley. You must interact with your in-game mailbox to trigger the questline that leads to the new areas.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim the Mailbox Package">
    Check your mailbox for a mysterious package containing childhood keepsakes and a **Tree Sprout**. This item is essential for opening the gateway.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plant the Tree Sprout">
    Find a suitable spot in your Valley or on the Floating Islands to plant the sprout. It will grow into a **Magical Glowing Tree Gateway**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter the Woods">
    Walk through the gateway to enter Honeyglow Woods. You will immediately encounter a strange fog that has separated the residents of the forest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Meet Winnie the Pooh">
    Your first objective is to find **Winnie the Pooh**. He will guide you through the initial steps of clearing the fog and restoring warmth to the land.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Gateway Flexibility">
The Magical Glowing Tree Gateway can be moved or removed using the Furniture mode at any time. Don't worry about its initial placement; you can redesign your Valley layout later.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

A core gameplay addition in the **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods release** is the beekeeping system. This is a multi-step production loop that rewards players with **Golden Honey**, a high-value resource used for crafting exclusive furniture and cooking themed recipes.

The process centers on the **Busy Bees’ House**, a new functional furniture item. To maximize production, you must understand the relationship between the bees and the flowers in the vicinity.

| Beekeeping Component | Function | Player Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Busy Bees’ House** | Collection point | Can be moved to any biome in the Valley. |
| **Flower Placement** | Powers production | Different flowers may affect honey yield rates. |
| **Pollination** | Growth boost | Bees increase the growth speed of nearby flowers. |
| **Golden Honey** | Crafting material | Used for the Honeyglow furniture set and cakes. |

**Honey-Based Recipes and Crafting:**
- **Warm Milk & Honey**: A cozy drink for energy restoration.
- **Honeycrunch Bar**: A sweet snack for villagers.
- **Pooh’s Birthday Cake**: A high-star dessert for friendship bonuses.
- **Honeypot Lamp**: A warm lighting fixture for cottagecore builds.
- **Cozy Honeyleaf Rug**: A nature-inspired decoration for indoor spaces.

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
Place your Busy Bees’ Houses near your main garden plots. The pollination boost will help your crops and flowers grow significantly faster, creating a dual-purpose farming zone.
</Callout>
