<Section icon={Target} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

The standout gameplay feature in Honeyglow Woods is the new beekeeping system. Unlike basic foraging, beekeeping requires active layout planning to maximize your resource output. Players must place Busy Bees' Houses and surround them with various flowers to kickstart production.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Busy Bees' Houses">
    Progress through the opening story quests in Honeyglow Woods to unlock your first Busy Bees' House structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Strategic Flower Placement">
    Place a variety of colorful flowers directly adjacent to the Busy Bees' House to allow the bees to pollinate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Harvest Golden Honey">
    Collect Golden Honey regularly from the houses. This serves as the core crafting ingredient for new recipes and furniture.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Boost Local Growth">
    Enjoy a passive growth speed increase for all flowers planted within the active radius of a pollinated bee house.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Farming Optimization">
You can move Busy Bees' Houses out of the expansion area and place them in any biome in your main Valley. This allows you to integrate honey production directly into your main farming layouts.
</Callout>

</Section>

Everyone drops an object into the river, and the first object to float past the finish line wins. It is a simple, charming way to build friendship levels with your new companions.
