<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unlocking the Four New Biomes and Everoak Tree">

The expansion map is divided into four distinct biomes arranged around the massive Everoak Tree. Unlocking these areas requires progressing through character quests and clearing the magical fog.

| Biome Name | Key Features | Primary Resources | Unlocking Condition |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Drowsybloom Acre** | Flower-filled meadow, starting zone | Wildflowers, early quest clues | Plant the gateway and enter |
| **Gloommeadow** | Misty swamp, Pooh Sticks bridge | Swamp materials, Critters | Complete Winnie the Pooh's initial quest |
| **Braveheart Grove** | Thick blooming forest, courage trial | Hardwood, rare plants | Assist Eeyore in overcoming the fog |
| **Nectar Apiary** | Giant honeycombs, beekeeping hub | Golden Honey, wild bees | Complete Piglet's courage questline |

At the center of these biomes stands the Everoak Tree. This central landmark houses intricate puzzles that you must solve to completely mend the land and uncover the source of the mysterious fog.

<Callout type="warning" title="Fog Navigation">
Do not attempt to wander deep into Braveheart Grove or Nectar Apiary before completing the corresponding character quests. The fog will teleport you back to the entrance if you have not unlocked the zone officially.
</Callout>

| Step | Action | Mechanical Benefit | Player Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | Unlock Beekeeping | Access Busy Bees' Houses | Progress the main story to unlock |
| **2** | Place Busy Bees' House | Establishes honey collection point | Can be moved to any Valley biome |
| **3** | Plant Nearby Flowers | Bees pollinate surrounding blooms | More flowers speed up honey production |
| **4** | Harvest Golden Honey | Gather the raw golden resource | Used in high-tier crafting and cooking |
| **5** | Boost Flower Growth | Pollinated areas grow flora faster | Passive buff applied to nearby plants |
