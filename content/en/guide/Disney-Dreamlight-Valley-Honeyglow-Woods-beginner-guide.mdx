<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

Beekeeping is the central resource system introduced in the Honeyglow Woods. By managing your hives and local flora, you can produce Golden Honey, which is required for crafting themed furniture and cooking new recipes.

| Beekeeping Step | Action Required | System Result |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Placement** | Set down a Busy Bees' House | Establishes the honey collection point |
| **2. Pollination** | Plant and water flowers nearby | Bees pollinate flowers to generate honey |
| **3. Harvesting** | Gather Golden Honey from the hive | Yields raw material for cooking and crafting |
| **4. Farming Boost** | Keep hives active and fully pollinated | Increases the growth speed of adjacent flowers |

<Callout type="success" title="Hive Portability">
Once you unlock and craft Busy Bees' Houses, you can move them out of the Honeyglow Woods. Place them in your main Valley biomes to create custom flower gardens with increased growth rates.
</Callout>

</Section>
