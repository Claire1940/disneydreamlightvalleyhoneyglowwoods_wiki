Starting the new expansion is simple, but it requires players to follow a specific sequence in their Valley to open the portal.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Retrieve the Mailbox Package">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plant the Tree Sprout">
    Find a suitable spot in your Valley and plant the Tree Sprout. Water it to watch it instantly grow into a magical glowing tree gateway.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Place the Gateway">
    Use the furniture edit mode to place the gateway anywhere in your main Valley or on the Floating Islands. You can move this gateway freely later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Step Through the Portal">
    Interact with the glowing tree to enter Honeyglow Woods. Once inside, you will find fast-travel portals linking back to Scrooge McDuck's Store and Chez Remy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Beekeeping & Golden Honey Mechanics">

Beekeeping is the core resource loop introduced in this expansion. By setting up managed hives, players can harvest Golden Honey to craft exclusive cozy furniture and cook themed desserts.

| Step | Action | Mechanical Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Place Busy Bees' House | Establishes the collection point for honey production |
| **2** | Plant Flowers Nearby | Bees pollinate nearby flowers, generating Golden Honey |
| **3** | Collect Golden Honey | Harvest the honey from the hive box to use in recipes |
| **4** | Boost Flower Growth | Active hives increase the growth speed of surrounding flowers |

<Callout type="success" title="Hive Portability">
Once unlocked, Busy Bees' Houses can be moved out of Honeyglow Woods and placed in any biome in your main Valley to speed up your standard flower farming routes.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Minigames, Critters, and Progression Goals">

Beyond the main story, Honeyglow Woods offers gentle side activities that help you bond with your new villagers.

### Pooh Sticks Mini-Game
Visit the bridge in the Gloommeadow to play Pooh Sticks with up to three villagers. Each participant drops a stick into the river, and the first stick to pass under the opposite side of the bridge wins. Winning or participating in this game grants daily friendship bonuses with the participating characters.

### Hedgehog Critters
The expansion introduces a new family of colorful hedgehogs. They can be fed wild berries to be unlocked as companions, and they feature exclusive cute poses when using the game's Photo Mode.
