<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beekeeping Mechanics and Golden Honey">

One of the most significant gameplay additions in Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods is the beekeeping system. This is a functional resource loop that provides materials for high-tier recipes and unique furniture crafting.

The core of this system is the **Busy Bees’ House**. Once you unlock this item through Pooh's early quests, you can begin your journey as a Valley apiarist.

| Step | Action | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Place Busy Bees' House | Establishes a collection point for honey. |
| **2** | Plant Flowers Nearby | Bees pollinate flowers to generate honey. |
| **3** | Wait for Production | The house fills with **Golden Honey** over time. |
| **4** | Harvest | Use honey for cooking or crafting. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Boost">
A fully pollinated Busy Bees’ House increases the growth speed of any flowers planted within its radius. Use this to farm rare flowers faster!
</Callout>

Golden Honey is used in several new 2026 recipes. These dishes offer high energy restoration and are often requested by villagers at Chez Remy.

| Recipe Name | Ingredients | Energy Restore |

| **Honeycrunch Bar** | Wheat, Sugarcane, Golden Honey | **2,200** |

The **Everoak Tree** itself acts as a central hub. Inside, you will find ancient puzzles that reveal why the Woods were separated from the rest of the Valley. Solving these puzzles is the key to completing the Adventure Pack's main storyline.
