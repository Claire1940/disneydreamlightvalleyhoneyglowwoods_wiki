<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mastery">

Beekeeping is the standout mechanic in the 2026 Adventure Pack. It is not just a cosmetic addition; it is a functional resource loop that produces Golden Honey, a required ingredient for high-tier recipes and Honeyglow furniture.

**The Beekeeping Loop:**
1.  **Place the House**: Craft or earn a Busy Bees' House and place it in any biome (including the main Valley).
2.  **Plant Flowers**: Surround the house with flowers. The variety of flowers does not change the honey type but affects the speed of production.
3.  **Pollination**: Bees will leave the house to pollinate nearby plants, which actually increases the growth speed of those flowers.
4.  **Harvest**: Collect Golden Honey from the house once the meter is full.

| Resource | Acquisition Method | Primary Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Golden Honey** | Harvested from Busy Bees' Houses | Cooking and Furniture Crafting |
| **Drowsybloom** | Foraged in Drowsybloom Acre | Beekeeping House Crafting |
| **Nectar** | Foraged near giant honeycombs | High-tier Honey Recipes |
| **Tree Sprout** | Initial Mailbox Reward | Opening the Expansion Gateway |

<Callout type="info" title="Efficiency Strategy">
Place your Busy Bees' Houses in a 3x3 grid surrounded by the rarest flowers you own. This maximizes the pollination bonus, helping you farm rare flowers and honey simultaneously.
</Callout>

</Section>
