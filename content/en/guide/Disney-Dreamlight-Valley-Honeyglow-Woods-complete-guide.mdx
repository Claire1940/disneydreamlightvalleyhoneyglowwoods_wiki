<Section icon={Swords} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

Beekeeping is the core resource loop introduced in Honeyglow Woods. By setting up managed hives, you can harvest Golden Honey, which is a required ingredient for high-tier cooking and crafting recipes.

| Step | Action | Practical Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Place Busy Bees' House | Establishes the physical collection point for your honey loop |
| **2** | Plant Flowers Nearby | Bees pollinate nearby flowers, which powers honey generation |
| **3** | Collect Golden Honey | Hives generate Golden Honey over time based on flower count |
| **4** | Boost Flower Growth | Active, pollinated hives increase the growth speed of local flora |

<Callout type="tip" title="Valley Beekeeping Setup">
You are not restricted to keeping your bees in the expansion area. Busy Bees' Houses can be picked up and placed in any biome back in your main Valley, making it easy to integrate honey farming into your daily routine.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Winnie the Pooh Characters and Activities">

The expansion centers around finding and befriending three classic characters from the Hundred Acre Wood. Each character has a dedicated questline and brings unique activities to the Valley.

*   **Winnie the Pooh**: Found immediately upon entering the Woods. He helps you clear the initial fog and teaches you the basics of honey harvesting.
*   **Eeyore**: Discovered wandering in the misty Gloommeadow. You must help him search for his lost tail to progress his friendship.
*   **Piglet**: Located deep within Braveheart Grove. His quests focus on overcoming fears and building courage in the dark forest.

### Pooh Sticks Mini-Game
Head to the wooden bridge in the Gloommeadow to play **Pooh Sticks** with up to four villagers. Each player drops a stick into the upstream water, and the first stick to drift past the finish line on the opposite side of the bridge wins. Playing this mini-game boosts friendship points with all participants.
