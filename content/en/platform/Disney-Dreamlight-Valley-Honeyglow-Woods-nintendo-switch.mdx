<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Farming">

Beekeeping is the central economy loop of the Honeyglow Woods expansion. By placing Busy Bees' Houses, you can generate Golden Honey, which is a required ingredient for high-tier cooking and crafting recipes.

| Mechanic | Input | Output | Primary Benefit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Busy Bees' House** | Wild or planted flowers | Golden Honey | Generates passive crafting resources |
| **Flower Pollination** | Active bee hives nearby | Faster flower growth | Speeds up rare flower farming |

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="1. Place Hives">
    Set up Busy Bees' Houses in clear, open areas. You can eventually move these hives back to your main Valley biomes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Surround with Flowers">
    Plant at least 4 to 6 flowers within a three-tile radius of each hive. The color and type of flower influence the visual bee activity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Harvest Honey">
    Check the hives regularly to collect Golden Honey. Fully pollinated setups will also cause surrounding flowers to respawn much faster.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="Recipe Synergy">
Save your Golden Honey to cook high-energy meals like Warm Milk & Honey, Honeycrunch Bars, and Pooh's Birthday Cake. These meals provide a yellow stamina bar boost, which makes running across the map faster.
</Callout>

</Section>
