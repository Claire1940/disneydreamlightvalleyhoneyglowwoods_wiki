### How Beekeeping and Golden Honey Work

The Beekeeping loop allows you to produce Golden Honey, a vital crafting and cooking ingredient. Follow this structure to optimize your honey production:

1. **Place a Busy Bees' House**: Unlock and craft these specialized structures, then place them in any biome (including the base game Valley).
2. **Surround with Flowers**: Plant and grow various flowers in the immediate vicinity of the Busy Bees' House to initiate pollination.
3. **Generate Golden Honey**: Active bees will automatically collect pollen and generate Golden Honey inside the house over time.
4. **Boost Growth Rates**: A fully pollinated Busy Bees' House provides a passive buff that increases the growth speed of all nearby flowers.

<Callout type="info" title="Beekeeping Strategy">
You can move Busy Bees' Houses to your main Valley biomes to easily integrate honey harvesting into your daily farming routes.
</Callout>

### Playing Pooh Sticks in the Gloommeadow

Pooh Sticks is a multiplayer mini-game located at the main bridge in the Gloommeadow biome. 

- **The Rules**: You and three selected villagers stand on the upstream side of the bridge and drop sticks into the river.
- **The Winner**: The player whose stick emerges on the downstream side of the bridge first wins the round.
- **The Reward**: Playing Pooh Sticks awards significant friendship progression points with all participating villagers, making it an excellent daily activity.

<Callout type="tip" title="Friendship Optimization">
Always bring villagers who are below level 10 to the Gloommeadow bridge. Pooh Sticks is one of the fastest ways to max out friendship levels without grinding gifts.
</Callout>
