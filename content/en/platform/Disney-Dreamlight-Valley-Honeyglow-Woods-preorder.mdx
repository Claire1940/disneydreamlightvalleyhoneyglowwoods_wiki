<Section icon={Map} title="How to Start the Honeyglow Woods Adventure">

Once you have purchased the expansion, you won't automatically teleport to the new area. You must follow a specific questline to open the gateway to the Hundred Acre Wood. This ensures that the story integrates naturally with your existing Valley progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check the Mailbox">
    Look for a mysterious package in your mailbox. It contains childhood keepsakes and a special **Tree Sprout**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plant the Sprout">
    Select a spot in your Valley or on a Floating Island to plant the Tree Sprout. Water it to watch it grow into a magical gateway.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cross the Threshold">
    Interact with the fully grown glowing tree to enter Honeyglow Woods. You will immediately be greeted by the strange fog.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Meet Winnie the Pooh">
    Follow the initial quest markers to find Pooh. He will guide you through clearing the first layer of fog in **Drowsybloom Acre**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Gateway Placement">
You can move the Magical Gateway tree using the Furniture Edit mode. If you don't like its initial placement, simply pick it up and move it to a more convenient location.
</Callout>
