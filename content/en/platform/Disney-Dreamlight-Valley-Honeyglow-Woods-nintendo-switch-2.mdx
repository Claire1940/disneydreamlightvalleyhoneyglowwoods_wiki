<Section icon={Swords} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

Beekeeping is the core gameplay loop introduced in the Honeyglow Woods expansion. Unlike gardening, beekeeping is a passive resource generation system that rewards careful spatial planning. You must balance the placement of **Busy Bees’ Houses** with a variety of flowers to maximize your yield of **Golden Honey**.

| Beekeeping Element | Function | Optimization Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Busy Bees’ House** | The collection point for honey. | Place in the center of a 5x5 flower grid. |
| **Pollination** | Increases honey production speed. | Use diverse flower types to attract more bees. |
| **Golden Honey** | Rare crafting and cooking resource. | Only produced when 3+ flower types are nearby. |
| **Growth Boost** | Increases nearby flower growth speed. | Place houses near your decorative gardens. |

The Golden Honey gathered from these houses is essential for the new 2026 recipe set. These recipes provide high energy restoration and are required for several Friendship Quests with Eeyore and Piglet.

<Callout type="info" title="Cross-Biome Beekeeping">
Once you unlock the Busy Bees’ House in the expansion, you can craft additional houses and place them in the base game Valley biomes like the Plaza or Peaceful Meadow.
</Callout>
