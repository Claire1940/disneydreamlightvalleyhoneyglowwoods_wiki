<Section icon={Trophy} title="Pooh Sticks Mini-Game and Hedgehog Critters">

Beyond quests and farming, the expansion offers relaxing side activities to enjoy with your villagers. Head over to the wooden bridge in the Gloommeadow to play **Pooh Sticks**, a classic physics-based mini-game.

- **How to Play**: Gather up to four villagers at the Gloommeadow bridge. Drop your chosen sticks into the river, watch them drift downstream, and see whose stick emerges on the other side of the bridge first.
- **Why Play**: Winning Pooh Sticks races awards significant Friendship Experience points with all participating villagers.

Additionally, keep an eye out for the new **Hedgehog critters** roaming the forest floors. These adorable colorful animals can be fed wild berries to earn their trust. Once befriended, they will accompany you on your travels and unlock cute new poses in Photo Mode.
