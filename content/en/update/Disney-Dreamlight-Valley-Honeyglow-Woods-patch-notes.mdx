<Section icon={Swords} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Recipes">

Beekeeping is the core resource loop introduced in the Honeyglow Woods patch notes. By setting up Busy Bees' Houses, you can generate Golden Honey to cook cozy new dishes and craft themed furniture.

| Crafting Item / Recipe | Category | Primary Ingredients |
|---|---|---|
| **Golden Honey** | Core Resource | Produced by Busy Bees' Houses near flowers |
| **Warm Milk & Honey** | Recipe | Milk, Golden Honey |
| **Honeycrunch Bar** | Recipe | Wheat, Golden Honey |
| **Pooh's Birthday Cake** | Recipe | Wheat, Butter, Egg, Golden Honey |
| **Honeypot Lamp** | Furniture | Clay, Golden Honey, Iron Ore |
| **Cozy Honeyleaf Rug** | Furniture | Fiber, Golden Honey, Green Leaves |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Busy Bees' Houses">
    Progress the story until you unlock the blueprint for the Busy Bees' House.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Plant Flowers Nearby">
    Place various flowers around the Busy Bees' House. The bees will pollinate them, which also boosts nearby flower growth speeds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Harvest Golden Honey">
    Collect Golden Honey regularly from the houses. You can also move these houses to any base game biome to customize your farm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Flower Optimization">
The more diverse the flowers you place around a Busy Bees' House, the faster your Golden Honey production and flower growth cycles will become.
</Callout>
