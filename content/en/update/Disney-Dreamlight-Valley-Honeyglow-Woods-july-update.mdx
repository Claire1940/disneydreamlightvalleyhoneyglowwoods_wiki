### Playing Pooh Sticks

Head to the wooden bridge in the **Gloommeadow** biome to play the Pooh Sticks mini-game. You can invite up to three other villagers to play.

- **How to Play**: Each player drops a stick over the upstream side of the bridge.
- **Winning**: The player whose stick flows under the bridge and crosses the downstream finish line first wins.
- **Reward**: Playing Pooh Sticks grants a massive boost to your active friendship levels.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Beekeeping, Golden Honey & Crafting Recipes">

Beekeeping is the central resource-gathering mechanic introduced in the **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods July update**. By managing your hives, you can harvest **Golden Honey** to craft themed furniture and cook delicious new meals.

### How to Start Beekeeping

1. **Place Busy Bees' Houses**: Set up these special hives in any biome (including your main Valley).
2. **Plant Flowers Nearby**: Place wild flowers around the hives. The more flowers nearby, the faster the bees pollinate.
3. **Harvest Golden Honey**: Collect the honey from the hives once they glow gold.
4. **Boost Flower Growth**: Active hives speed up the growth rate of all nearby flowers.

### July Update Recipe & Crafting Book

Use your harvested Golden Honey at any cooking station or crafting bench to unlock these new items.

| Item Name | Item Type | Ingredients / Materials Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Cozy Honeyleaf Rug** | Furniture | Fiber, Fabric, Drowsybloom Flowers |
