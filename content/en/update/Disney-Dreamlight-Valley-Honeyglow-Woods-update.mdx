<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Mechanics">

Beekeeping is the core resource loop introduced in the **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods** update. By setting up managed hives, you can produce **Golden Honey**, which serves as a vital crafting material and cooking ingredient for expansion-exclusive recipes.

This system relies on placing appropriate flora near your hives to maximize production speed and quality. Follow this guide to establish your first honey farm:

| Beekeeping Step | Action Required | Gameplay Effect |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **1. Place Hive** | Build and place a **Busy Bees' House** in an open area | Establishes the collection point for bees |
| **2. Plant Flowers** | Arrange active flowers in the immediate tiles surrounding the hive | Bees pollinate the flowers, boosting honey production |
| **3. Harvest Honey** | Interact with the Busy Bees' House when the honey icon appears | Yields **Golden Honey** and rare beeswax materials |
| **4. Boost Growth** | Leave active hives surrounded by fully pollinated flowers | Increases the natural growth rate of all nearby crops and flowers |

<Callout type="success" title="Biome Portability">
Once you unlock and craft additional Busy Bees' Houses, you can move them out of the Honeyglow Woods. Place them in any biome in your main Valley to integrate honey farming into your primary agricultural layouts.
</Callout>

</Section>
