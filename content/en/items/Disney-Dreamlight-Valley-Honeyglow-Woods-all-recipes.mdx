<Section icon={Target} title="Beekeeping and Golden Honey Generation">

Before you can begin cooking the **Disney Dreamlight Valley Honeyglow Woods all recipes**, you must establish a functional apiary. Golden Honey cannot be bought; it must be cultivated using Busy Bees' Houses.

Follow these steps to set up a high-yield honey farm in any biome.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Busy Bees' Houses">
    Progress through the main story quests in Honeyglow Woods until you receive your first Busy Bees' House blueprint.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Strategic Placement">
    Place the Busy Bees' House in an open area. You can place these houses in the new biomes or move them to the base game Valley.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Flower Arrangement">
    Plant and arrange multiple flowers directly around the perimeter of the house to trigger the pollination mechanic.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Harvest Golden Honey">
    Interact with the Busy Bees' House periodically to collect the generated Golden Honey for your cooking and crafting needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Pollination Range">
Flowers must be placed within a three-tile radius of the Busy Bees' House. If flowers are too far away, honey production will halt.
</Callout>

</Section>
